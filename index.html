Step 5 of 8
Connect Your AI Coach
Power your coach with Google's Gemini AI — it's free.
With an AI key, your coach can estimate macros from food photos, give personalized advice based on your actual data, and help you plan meals around real-life events. Without it, you'll get basic offline responses only.
How to get your free API key:
  1. Visit aistudio.google.com/apikey
  2. Sign in with your Google account
  3. Click Create API Key
  4. Copy the key and paste it below
Your key is stored only on your device in your browser's local storage and is never sent to HeatFit servers — it goes directly from your browser to Google's API.
